Campaign Characters

Meet the heroes, villains, and everything in between. These characters come from different campaigns, some past and some new:

  • Varic Grimhand – A cunning spellcaster from the Feywild.
  • Strider – A ranger with a mysterious past and sharp instincts.
  • Tasha – A traveling jester with surprising magical power.
  • Kyle Dawnsinger – A bard whose music inspires hope and hides deadly secrets.
  • Adam Wyrmshade – A brooding dragonborn who hides a tragic past beneath his scales.
  • Bjorn – A stoic warrior with a heart forged in the frozen north.
  • Veros The Red – A noble wizard whose power is unmatched.
