Main Lore

The Goblin's Grotto world is a fractured realm of ancient magic, forgotten kingdoms, and whispering forests. Long ago, a magical rift known as the Goblin's Grotto was opened, allowing fey creatures and arcane forces to leak into the world.

The land is ruled by a hidden monarchy in the capital Johpiel, under the guise of a wizard-led council. Shadows move behind the scenes, and secretive spy factions work for unseen masters.

Map of Valeria

The realm of Valeria is a vast continent ruled in name by the City of Johpiel, a powerful magical capital. However, real power rests with its Council of Archmages—an enigmatic assembly of the realm’s greatest spellcasters. Beneath their watchful eyes, a secretive network of spies operates across the land, ensuring the balance of magical authority remains unchallenged.
