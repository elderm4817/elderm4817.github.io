Map of Valeria

The realm of Valeria is a vast continent ruled in name by the City of Johpiel, a powerful magical capital. However, real power rests with its Council of Archmages—an enigmatic assembly of the realm’s greatest spellcasters. Beneath their watchful eyes, a secretive network of spies operates across the land, ensuring the balance of magical authority remains unchallenged. Would you go against this rule or would you work along side them to ensure their authority remain unchallenged?
